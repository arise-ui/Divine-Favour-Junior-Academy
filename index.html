Who We Are

About Divine Favour Junior Academy

A community of learners, teachers, and families united by a shared belief in the power of education to transform lives.

Divine Favour Junior Academy is a dedicated learning institution located in Taveta, Kenya. We are committed to providing quality education under the Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC), nurturing each child's unique potential in a safe and stimulating environment.

Our school welcomes learners from all backgrounds at Pre-school, Primary, and Junior Secondary levels. We believe that every child deserves a strong foundation — academically, morally, and socially — and we work tirelessly to deliver exactly that.

  • CBC-aligned curriculum from PP1 to Grade 9
  • Experienced and dedicated teaching staff
  • Safe, nurturing, and inclusive school environment
  • Strong focus on character and values formation
